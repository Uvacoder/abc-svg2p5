svg2p5

Converter

svg2p5 converts SVG markup into p5.js canvas code. It is based on Professor Cloudâ€™s SVG to HTML5 Converter and the Canvg.js library.

    What svg2p5 is pretty good at:
  • Bezier-based forms, like those made with the pen tool in GIMP.
  • Path elements with many points.
  • Stroke caps and stroke joins.
  • Color attributes in HEX and RGB.
    What svg2p5 sucks at (for now):
  • Native elements, like circles.
  • Color gradients.
  • Embedded text.
  • Multi-layered, multi-path SVGs, especially with stroke involved.

And many more. Consider filing an issue or contributing to the project.
